About Me

Bruno Goretti is the alias of Gor Arshakyan, a digital artist with nearly a decade of experience creating bold, stylized visual content for clients in the music, entertainment, and creative industries.

I specialize in poster-style illustrations, concept art, and custom digital artwork designed for everything from music merchandise to social media visuals. Driven by a lifelong passion for video games and visual storytelling, my work draws heavy inspiration from the iconic Grand Theft Auto loading screen illustrations—particularly classics like Vice City Stories, San Andreas, and Liberty City Stories.

My unique art style pays homage to this era, blending nostalgic influences with modern techniques to create imagery that feels timeless yet strikingly fresh. Over the years, I've collaborated with independent musicians, content creators, and small studios, developing distinctive aesthetics tailored to each project's tone and audience.

Whether it's a gritty urban character piece or a vibrant fictional poster, my work is crafted to grab attention and tell a compelling story. I'm open to new collaborations—from personal commissions to brand visuals, album covers, or conceptual world-building.

If you're looking for distinct, stylized, high-impact artwork, let's connect and create something unforgettable.
